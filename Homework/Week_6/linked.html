This is a linked view visualization with a map and the happy planet index rank.
The user can move with the mouse over the countries to see the happy planet index as well as the ranking.


When the user clicks on a specific country, two bar charts will be provided
The first barchart shows the life expectancy rate of that country against the global life expectancy rate.
The second barchart shows the average well being of that country against the global well being rate.
